# ESN GO - Project Vision

## Mission

Help every Erasmus student visiting Türkiye discover cities like a local through an intuitive digital platform.

## The Problem

Erasmus students often struggle to:
- Find reliable city information in their language
- Discover authentic local experiences
- Plan efficient travel routes
- Connect with other students' experiences

## Our Solution

**ESN GO** - A comprehensive platform featuring:
- Multi-language city guides
- Curated tour packages
- Student reviews and ratings
- Route planning and favorites
- ESNcard integration for discounts

## Why It Matters

Türkiye hosts thousands of Erasmus students annually. ESN GO will enhance their experience, support ESN Türkiye's mission, and showcase Turkish cities professionally.

**Vision**: Every Erasmus student has an unforgettable Turkish experience.
